Our website (this page) does not use cookies or tracking technologies. Third-party platforms you navigate to may use cookies as described in their own privacy policies.

6. Data Retention

We retain any email correspondence for up to 12 months, after which it is deleted. We do not retain any platform engagement data beyond what the platforms themselves retain.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@pulsevault.co.

8. Children's Privacy

PulseVault content is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
